About SBCTF

SBCTF (Stone Bridge Capture the Flag) is a cyber security capture the flag competition made by high schoolers for high schoolers. It covers topics such as coding, steganography, and networking.

contacts

Who Can Compete

All Stone Bridge High School students are allowed to compete. You do not have to be in a computer science or computer math class to compete! Students can register below.
